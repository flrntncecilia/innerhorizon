Between the streets and stories, I find pieces of myself. Traveling is more than a habit; it’s a way of connecting with the world and healing through movement. Through this blog, I want to share the fragments of what I see, live and feel. Maybe, you’ll find something of yourself in it too.

What's my point here?

I believe that traveling is more than visiting places — it's about collecting moments, understanding cultures, and reshaping who we are. This blog is a space where I narrate not only where I’ve been, but why those places changed me. If there’s one thing I’ve learned, it’s that every corner holds a story.
