An urban manifest

Welcome! My name is Cecília and I'm just a girl who loves living.

I travel by myself, exploring the concrete, the corners, the coincidences and living the best of each city. Every trip is cultural baggage, bravery and unlikely encounters.

If you're an urban person, you're in the right place. Cities have something special in them; something that catches my attention and makes me interested in finding out more about every place I go.

Here, I'll share details about my trips, what I've done, what you could do in each place, and traveling tips for young, bold women. Feel free to dive deep into my horizons.

Between the streets

Every city welcomes me in its own way. Being a young woman who loves traveling and meeting new people gives me the best experiences ever.

Between the streets, I get to know more people, learn from their stories, and share my own. That's what keeps me moving.
