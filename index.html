An urban manifest

Traveling, for me, has never been about luxury. It's about last-minute bookings, sharing a seat with strangers, and finding beauty in the noise of concrete jungles.

Urban experiences, possible journeys, unlikely encounters, cultural enrichment. In my reality, traveling is an accessible experience. It's crossing cities as if crossing through oneself. I carry these perspectives as a young Brazilian woman, full-time worker, and curious soul who craves real experiences and carries the world in a suitcase.

This blog was born out of a desire to reaffirm our possibilities in this big world. And if you also feel that there’s more out there waiting beyond the storefronts, then this space is yours too.

Feel free to dive deep into my horizons.

Between the streets

Here, the streets are the stage, photography is memory, the mishaps are part of the route, improvisation becomes a path, and freedom is measured in bold steps as we explore spaces filled with countless other stories.

It’s between the streets that life escapes the script, where maps fail and the gaze discovers. The destination is now, and our privilege is to feel it.

Welcome to a place where struggle becomes poetry, and the asphalt has a soul.
